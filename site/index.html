FAQ

Can I self-host everything?

Yes. The open-source core runs locally and can be deployed anywhere.

Is this just another email MCP server?

No. Nerve is a runtime: threads, memory, triage, extraction, policy drafting.

Do you support custom domains?

Yes on Cloud (and self-host obviously). Local dev works via tunneling later.
